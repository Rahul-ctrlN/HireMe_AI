# Project: Voice-Based Job Matching Platform

## Problem
Daily-wage workers struggle to find nearby jobs quickly.  
Contractors also find it difficult to locate available workers on time.  
Most hiring happens through calls or WhatsApp and is unorganized.  
Many workers cannot use complex job apps due to language or literacy barriers.

## Solution
A voice-based AI platform that connects workers and contractors instantly.  
Workers can speak in their local language to search for jobs.  
Contractors can post job requirements easily.  
AI matches nearby workers and sends real-time alerts.

## Users
- Daily wage workers  
- Contractors  
- Small business owners  
- Local service providers  

## Key Features
- Two login system: Worker and Contractor  
- Voice-based job search using NLP  
- Quick job posting by contractors  
- AI-based worker matching  
- Real-time job notifications  
- Feedback and skill improvement suggestions  

## Impact
- Faster hiring  
- More job opportunities  
- Increased income for workers  
- Organized local job ecosystem
